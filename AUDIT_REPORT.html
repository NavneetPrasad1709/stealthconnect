/signup page showed "Sign In" as the browser tab title
Fixed by giving /signup its own server-side metadata: now reads "Create your account — StealthConnect AI" in the tab, bookmarks, and Google snippets.

C2
Price calculator showed different prices than the published volume table
At qty 50, the calculator quoted ~$0.77/phone while the table said $0.90. Root cause: the calculator used linear interpolation; the table uses step-pricing. C3
Testimonials used Unsplash stock photos attributed to Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, Notion, and Intercom employees
Legal risk: FTC (US), EU Unfair Commercial Practices Directive, and UK DMCC 2024 all prohibit fabricated testimonials attributed to real companies. Fixed by removing all real company names, replacing Unsplash photos with CSS initials avatars — no image CDN, no reverse-image-search exposure.
